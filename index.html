// About

PWNAGE ESPORTS B.V.

Pwnage Esports is a Netherlands-based company building infrastructure for the competitive gaming industry. Our flagship product, Goblam, is an online esports tournament system designed to serve organisers, teams, and players at every level of competition.

We believe competitive gaming deserves purpose-built tooling — not patched-together spreadsheets and Discord bots. Goblam delivers a professional-grade experience from registration to results.
